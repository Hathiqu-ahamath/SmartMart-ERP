# User Manual

## User Roles

### Admin
Full system access - manage users, roles, products, inventory, sales, and reports.

### Cashier
Access to POS billing and sales history. Can create and view sales transactions.

### Storekeeper
Manages inventory, receives goods (GRN), adjusts stock, creates purchase orders.

### Manager
Views reports, approves purchase orders, monitors business performance.

## Modules

### Dashboard
Shows key metrics: today's revenue, transactions, low stock alerts, expiring products. Includes sales trend chart and recent activity.

### Products
Full CRUD with search, pagination, barcode support, and stock status indicators.

### Categories
Organize products into groups for easier management.

### Suppliers
Manage vendor information and view purchase history.

### Purchase Orders
Create, approve, and track orders. Automatic stock update on receipt.

### Goods Received Notes (GRN)
Receive goods against purchase orders with partial receipt support.

### Inventory
View stock levels, adjust quantities, track movements, identify low stock/expired items.

### POS (Point of Sale)
Product grid, shopping cart, discount/tax handling, receipt generation.

### Reports
Daily and monthly sales with profit calculation, inventory valuation reports.

## Notifications
- Low stock alerts when quantity <= reorder level
- Expiry alerts for products expiring within 30 days
